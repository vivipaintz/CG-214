Vivian Raquel Valentin is a renowned artist based in Brooklyn, New York. With her aggressive, intuitive and raw pieces, it's quite apparent that this young artist leaves her heart on the canvas.



Vivian has studied Fine Arts at the Fashion Institute of Technology and the Art Students League of New York. Both establishments have assisted Vivian in not only mastering artistic techniques but has helped nourish and develop her creative process. She is a multidimensional artist that has over three years experience in Art Therapy and has worked in the fashion industry doing technical design.



Creativity and intelligence are just scratching the surface to what this ambitious woman has to offer. Some of her influences are artists like; Jackson Pollack, Willem de Kooning, Frida Kahlo and the words of Pablo Neruda. Her greatest influence has always been her mother. A strong woman who gave Vivian her depth of emotion and who always taught her to dream big and believe in herself.

"I speak through my art, and I would ultimately like to make connections with good people whose ambition and passion matches my own." V.R.V
