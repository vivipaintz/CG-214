    • Brazilian Jiu-jitsu has blown up across the world and especially in the United States thanks to mixed martial arts organizations such as UFC, Bellator, Strikeforce and EBI.


    Organizations like UFC combine fighting styles and that's why it's called mixed martial arts. MMA fighters need to be skilled in multiple fields such as muay thai, wrestling, boxing, judo and brazilian jiu jitsu. The best MMA fighters are jiu-jitsu black belts. A fan favorite is Ronda Rousey who is an olympic level judo fighter. When she transitioned into MMA she cleaned house because of her technique in jiu-jitsu. No one could match her on the ground.
